RiFi is a native wireless media controller and gamepad for MacOS Devices.

Features

 

Instant Pairing

Rifi uses Apples native api's to pair and connect to your Mac, it's super reliable, works with or without internet connection, it is very secure and has low latency.

 

Design

Made using SwiftUI, RiFi has a very modern design, the app is incredibly user friendly, with haptic feedback and gestures support. Even the button react to your taps in a pleasing way.

 

Works with almost anything

Not only you can control Apple Music and Quicktime you can also control third party media players like VLC, IINA and much more without having to install any other additional plugins


Supported Players

  • VLC Player
  • IINA
  • Youtube
  • Apple Music
  • Quicktime
  • Netflix
  • Hulu
  • ..etc
Techincally RiFi supports everything as you can always fall back to KeyBoard Controls :D
